Common questions

Before you choose a provider.

These questions help homeowners compare independent wildlife providers more clearly before scheduling or hiring.

Is WILD GUARD a wildlife removal company?

No. WILD GUARD is an independent matching platform that helps homeowners connect with local wildlife service providers. We do not perform wildlife removal, exclusion, inspection, or repair services directly.

How do I compare local wildlife providers?

Ask about licensing where required, insurance, humane methods, inspection process, exclusion recommendations, pricing, timing, and follow-up policies.

What affects wildlife service pricing?

Pricing may depend on animal type, entry points, attic or crawlspace access, removal complexity, exclusion work, cleanup needs, location, and provider availability.

How do I know if a provider serves my area?

WILD GUARD can help route your request based on location, but homeowners should confirm service area and availability directly with the provider.
