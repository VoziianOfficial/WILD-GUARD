Prepare your request

Small details can make provider matching clearer.

You do not need to know the exact animal type. Before using the form, it helps to think through what you noticed, where it happened, and whether the activity is new or repeated.

Sounds or timing Scratching, movement, chirping, nighttime noise, or activity at dusk.
Location around home Attic, roofline, vents, chimney, crawlspace, garage, yard, or exterior gaps.
Visible signs Droppings, tracks, nesting material, stains, chewed areas, or damaged vents.
Questions to ask Inspection fee, scope, timing, license, insurance, exclusion, and follow-up terms.
