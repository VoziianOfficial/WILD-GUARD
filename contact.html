Contact questions

Before you submit a request.

These answers explain how the matching request works and what WILD GUARD does not directly provide.

Is there a cost to submit a request through WILD GUARD?

WILD GUARD is a free service to assist homeowners in connecting with local service providers. Providers may have their own inspection fees, quote policies, or service charges, which homeowners should confirm directly.

Will WILD GUARD send its own technicians?

No. WILD GUARD does not have wildlife technicians and does not perform direct services. Any provider contacted through the platform is independent.

What details should I include in the form?

Include the type of animal if known, noises, droppings, nesting material, visible entry points, location around the home, and how long the activity has been happening.

Can I call instead of filling out the form?

Yes. You can use the phone CTA to start a request. The same aggregator-safe process applies: WILD GUARD helps with matching, while independent providers discuss service details directly.

What should I verify before scheduling?

Verify license and insurance requirements, inspection fees, service scope, humane methods, pricing, exclusions, service area, and follow-up terms directly with each provider.
