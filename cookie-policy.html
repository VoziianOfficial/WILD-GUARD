Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how WILD GUARD may use cookies, pixels, tags, scripts, local storage, and similar technologies to support website functionality, analytics, advertising measurement, and provider-matching operations.

1. Overview

WILD GUARD is a free website that helps homeowners connect with independent local wildlife service providers. This Cookie Policy explains how tracking and storage technologies may be used when you visit the website, submit a form, click a contact link, or interact with website features.

Cookies help the website remember certain choices, improve functionality, measure page performance, support advertising attribution, and understand how visitors interact with provider-matching content.

2. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on a browser or device when a website is visited. Similar technologies may include pixels, tags, scripts, local storage, session storage, device identifiers, and analytics tools.

Some cookies may be set directly by WILD GUARD. Others may be set by third-party vendors that provide analytics, advertising, measurement, form processing, call tracking, or website functionality services.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies support core website functionality, such as security, navigation, form operation, consent preferences, and basic page behavior.

Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use the website, which pages are visited, how users move through the site, and where improvements may be needed.

Advertising and Measurement Cookies

Advertising or measurement technologies may help evaluate campaign performance, understand referral sources, measure form activity, and support relevant advertising or lead-routing operations.

Preference Cookies

Preference cookies or local storage may remember choices such as cookie consent decisions, interface preferences, or previously selected form values where applicable.

4. Third-Party Technologies

WILD GUARD may work with third-party vendors, partners, analytics providers, advertising platforms, call-tracking services, hosting providers, or lead-processing partners. These parties may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

These technologies may help process matching requests, measure website performance, identify referral sources, prevent misuse, and support communication with independent local providers.

5. Your Cookie Choices

You can accept or decline cookies through the website cookie notice where available. You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Browser settings usually allow you to block cookies, delete cookies, or receive alerts when cookies are being used.

If you block or delete cookies, some website features may not function as intended, including consent preference storage, form behavior, analytics, and certain matching request functionality.

6. Local Storage and Consent Preferences

The website may use local storage to remember whether you accepted or declined the cookie notice. This prevents the notice from appearing repeatedly after you make a choice.

If you clear browser data or use another browser or device, the cookie notice may appear again because the prior preference may no longer be available.

7. Updates to This Cookie Policy

WILD GUARD may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in website features, technology, vendor relationships, legal requirements, or operational practices. Updates may be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
